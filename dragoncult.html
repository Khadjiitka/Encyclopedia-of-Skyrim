Dragon Priests

Dragon Priests were once the mortal rulers of ancient Skyrim, chosen by dragons to lead humanity under their dominion. Gifted with powerful masks and immense magical strength, they became symbols of fear and reverence.

After the Dragon War, many were entombed alongside their undead followers, Draugr. Some remain dormant, while others awaken when the Dragonborn or Alduin stirs ancient magics. Their masks contain unique enchantments and represent different aspects of their former power.

Each Dragon Priest was a tyrant, a sorcerer-king, and a loyal servant of the dragons. Their legacy persists in the ancient Nordic ruins scattered across Skyrim.
